News About The Environment

  • Could cutting down trees may be the end of the world we know?

    Everyday things such as pencils, paper, and more are all produce by trees. Although we think everything is fine and keep continuing on with our daily lives, but it is really not. Trees are our source of oxygen, which allows us to breathe, but every day, thousands of trees are cut down. This makes a widespread effect across forests and other biomes filled with trees. As a result of cutting down trees, oxygen sources may not be availble. Due to this, many companies now rise to bring trees back to the Earth to make it how it always was. If you want to help their cause, consider donating to them, usually it is only one dollar to plant a tree! Also, you might be able to learn how to garden by planting multiple trees, making a new hobby while also helping the world. Especially at a time like now, the Earth needs your help, and don't be afraid to speak up. Every bit counts!
